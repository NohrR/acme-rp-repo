Our Story

ACME was established in 2002 in Shenzhen, China. Starting small, we made it our mission to become one of the best competitors in the precision machining market. After nearly 30 years of hard work and hundreds of happy customers, we've grown to a company that employs over 100 employees that strive to deliver the best product we can possibly make.

Our team is constantly striving to improve our technique by using the newest cutting edge technologies that enable precise machining possible. Thanks to this, we've been able to service customers globally in several different fields, such as medical, automotive, industrial, chemical and even military.
